About Us

KHWALI is a steel fabrication company in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, specialising in high-quality, durable, and custom steel solutions. We serve individuals, contractors, and organisations in residential, commercial, and institutional projects, including homes, schools, hospitals, and industrial facilities. At KHWALI, we prioritise precision, strength, and reliability, ensuring every project meets safety standards and client expectations.
